About the Game

Toy Match: Royal Magic Blast lets players match 3 or more toys to clear puzzles.

Levels get trickier as you go, throwing new challenges your way like blocks, chains, and magic boosters. Players aim to complete objectives while unlocking rewards and magical surprises.

Core Mechanics

The matches need to feel amazing - like when you clear half the board in one move and can't help but grin.

  • Swap and match 3 or more toys

  • Use boosters and special items to clear obstacles

  • Hit your level goals to move forward

  • Unlock new boards and magical environments

Features

  • The graphics are super vibrant - kind of like if your old toy box came to life

  • Hundreds of levels with increasing challenge

  • Tons of power-ups for when you're completely stumped and need to blow stuff up

  • Special events and limited-time challenges

  • Progression rewards and unlockable content

How to Play

It's super easy to learn, looks gorgeous when you're playing, and has that dangerous 'okay just one more...' addiction factor.

  • Select and swap adjacent toys

  • Match 3+ toys of the same type to clear them

  • Time your combos and boosters just right

  • Complete all objectives before running out of moves
