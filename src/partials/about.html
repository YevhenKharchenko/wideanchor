THE STORY OF JOE

Joe was a farmer with responsibilities — crops to tend, family to support, and little time for dreams. But inside him lived an artist.

He practiced drawing in sand and mud, experimenting with his old cropduster plane whenever he had a spare moment. Encouraged by a local pilot-artist, Joe committed to daily practice. Slowly, his skill improved. His sky drawings gained attention, and he earned the title of Best Local Artist of the Three Windmills.

But success only fueled his ambition.

Determined to become the best in the state — and eventually the country — Joe left home to compete. Along the way, he faced fierce rivals, including a cheating pilot who broke the rules with water cannons and destructive weapons. Joe’s plane was damaged, but his spirit remained unbroken.

He repaired, upgraded, and returned stronger.

Now it’s your turn to join Joe in the skies.
